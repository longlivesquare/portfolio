My Story

I began my programmming journey at a young age. My father worked in Tech Services for Fresno Unified School District so we had a computer in the house. I remember playing games on Prodigy and I wrote a "How To" on changing a hard drive for writing assignment in second grade. Eventually I got my dad's old computer where I got to experience DOS.

I had my first experience with programming in elementary school with the Logo programming language. In high school I took a BASIC programming class. Yes, that BASIC. I knew that I wanted to be a programmer. I then went and got an A.S. in Computer Information Technology from Fresno City and a B.S. in Computer Science from California State University, Fresno.
