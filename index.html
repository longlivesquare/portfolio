My Story

I have been interested in technology from a young age. My father worked in tech services for Fresno Unified School District. I remember playing games on Proodigy. I wrote a "How To" on changing a hard drive for an assignment in first grade.

I began my programming journey in elementary school when we did Logo programming in computer class. As a freshmen in high school I learned BASIC. I knew that I wanted to be a programmer.

Unfortunately, knowing what you want and doing what you want aren't always the same thing. Due to bad study habits I lost focus getting to college. After too many years and too many colleges, I finally got my B.S. in Computer Science from Fresno State.

After all that time spent in school, I was a bit burnt out on computers. This combined with my grandma passing away shortly after my graduation, I needed a break. Here I am now, taking some classes at Geekwise getting my confidence back.
